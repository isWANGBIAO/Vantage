# Goals Template

This public template contains generic goals only. Replace it locally with your
own goals if needed.

## Body And Health

- Long-term priority: health, sustainable training, and reliable sleep.
- Appearance target: 穿衣好看薄肌目标, including 肩背挺, 腰不粗, 有点胸和手臂,
  能跑能跳, 身体轻, and 有线条但不夸张.
- Avoid extreme dieting, dehydration, unsafe training loads, or sacrificing sleep
  for short-term metrics.

## Work

- Prefer evidence-based planning.
- Separate recorded facts from suggestions.
- Mark missing or uncertain evidence as 无法确认.
