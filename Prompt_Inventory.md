# Inventory Template

This public template avoids private inventory details. Store real asset,
subscription, finance, and device records outside Git.

## Example Asset Ownership Rules

- Personal assets must be recorded separately from borrowed, lab, school, or
  company assets.
- Do not treat shared or borrowed devices as personal purchase recommendations.
- Example placeholder labels retained for compatibility tests:
  - 实验室资产：AMD Ryzen 9950X + RTX 5090
  - 实验室资产：无独显轻薄本
